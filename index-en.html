About

Our mission

ibada was created with a simple vision: to facilitate the tracking and improvement of Muslims' religious practice in their daily lives.

In a world where we measure everything - our steps, our sleep, our diet - we created a tool that allows us to measure what really matters: our relationship with Allah and our spiritual journey.

The app is developed by 3ibada, a French company dedicated to innovation in the field of spiritual technologies. Our commitment: respect your privacy and provide you with tools based on authentic sources.
